Timekeeping

Allows users to record working time, view working hours, check work schedules, and request leave.


Personal Information

Allows users to view and update personal information such as address, phone number, email, and bank account information.


Search and Calendar View

Allows users to search for information about colleagues, departments, and view personal or group work schedules.


Company Policy Information

Provides information about company policies and procedures such as leave regulations, work schedules, and other regulations.

Notifications and Messages

Shows notifications about upcoming events, information about new work assignments, and messages from colleagues or managers.


Personal Statistics

Displays reports and statistics on working time, overtime hours, and vacation days used.


Help and Support

Link to a help page or provide contact information so users can get support if needed.


Quick Links

Set quick links to features like asking for time off, timekeeping, viewing work, and finding coworkers.
